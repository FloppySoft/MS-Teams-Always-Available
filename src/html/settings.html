MS Teams: Always Available

This Chrome extension works by calling Microsoft's servers every 30 seconds. As long as you have a Chrome tab open and are logged into https://teams.microsoft.com, you will remain available.

Subscription

This Chrome extension requires a $2 a month subscription. Note: After changing subscription status, reload settings by clicking the extension's icon and confirm your payment status.
